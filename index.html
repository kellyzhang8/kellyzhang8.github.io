About

Hi! I am Kaili(Kelly) Zhang, a Ph.D. student at Texas A&M University. My research interests lie in urban studies, climate justice, ecological footprint, and machine learning. I study how climate, ecological, and socioeconomic processes shape cities and communities, with an emphasis on spatial data science and policy-relevant evidence.

Before joining Texas A&M University, I earned my master's and bachelor's degrees in Urban and Rural Planning from Wuhan University. I am always happy to connect — feel free to reach out via email.
